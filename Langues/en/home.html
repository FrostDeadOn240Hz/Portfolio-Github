Game overview :

Inazuma Eleven: Victory Road marks the grand return of Level-5's cult franchise. More than just a sequel, it's the culmination of 15 years of soccer history, combining RPG strategy and high-octane action..

An unprecedented scenario :

Forget Raimon for a moment. The story takes place in Nagasaki and follows Unmei Sasanami, an unconventional protagonist who can no longer play soccer. As a genius strategist, he must rebuild a forgotten club to challenge the reigning champions.

Chronicle mode :

Every fan's ultimate dream: recruit over 4,500 characters from every era of the series! Create your “Dream Team” by choosing from players from the original saga, GO, and Ares/Orion to take on the whole world.

Reinvented gameplay :

The match system has been completely redesigned to offer total freedom (“Free-Form”). Clashes “Focus,” tension gauge, and spectacular super techniques: the gameplay is designed to be as intense with a controller in hand as it is on a touchscreen.
